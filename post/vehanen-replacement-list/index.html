Potential Vehanen Replacements

With die Eisbären Berlin Goalkeeper Petri Vehanen coming up nearing retirement and many fearing this to be his last season, I decided to do a fantasy search for potential replacements in case he decides not to renew his contract. I’ve used the data from the leagues analysis to come up with projected stats for each Goalkeeper. My search’s aim was to find a Goalkeeper who would be a good fit to do 50⁄50 load share with Marvin Cüpper as other DEL teams have been doing this and have been having good results with it and it helps with the development of Cüpper lining him up to be an overall starter for die Eisbären.

My search started with finding Goalkeepers whose contract runs out at the end of the year and then looking for Goalkeepers who have good stats. I kept my search to leagues other than the DEL. However, I have added a DEL pick, and I’ve also added my overall pick for next year in the event that we’re lucky enough to keep Vehanen for another year.
