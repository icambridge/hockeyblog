Goalie Transfer Sources

As part of my analysis of Goalkeeping within the Europe leagues, I decided it would be fun to find out where leagues get most of their Goalkeepers from. My approach was to get every Goalkeeper on EliteProspects and then track their movement between leagues via their stats. Any league that had less than five transfers were grouped within “Others”.

Interestingly there is only one league that gets more of its goalies from other teams within itself is the DEL. Most other league’s most prominent source is their junior leagues.

The league that has the most transfers from other countries is Liiga; this could be explained by Finnish Goalkeepers returning home from other leagues.

Unsurprisingly, the largest destination for NHL goalies is the KHL, with the SHL and Liiga being the other two significate destination for NHL goalies.
